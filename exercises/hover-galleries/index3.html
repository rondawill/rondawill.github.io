New Year's is always the first day of a new year. People celebrate different. Some go to church while others throw parties.

HAPPY NEW YEAR

What Is New Years

As the clock approaches midnight on December 31, people gather at public squares, clubs, or private parties. They count down the seconds together, shouting “10, 9, 8…” until the stroke of midnight. When the clock strikes 12, there are cheers, hugs, and kisses.

CELEBRATE

Celebrate New Years

The confetti in Times Square has thousands of people's wishes written on them. In 2015 "wishfetti" became a part of the tradition. People write their wishes for the new year and submit them to the Wish Wall in Times Square (or online) and those wishes are turned into the confetti that falls over the crowd at midnight.

NEW YEAR

Fun Fact

Times Square first hosted a New Year's Eve celebration in 1904 with a giant fireworks show but when city leaders banned fireworks a few years later, the ball drop tradition was born in 1907.
