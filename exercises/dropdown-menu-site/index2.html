HUNNY

Hunny is an adorable rabbit! He’s a 12 lb rabbit who is 10-months-old. He’s a playful little rabbit who adores people and has never met a toy or a treat he didn’t love. He’s the perfect companion for a kid who needs a buddy. Hunny is still learning to trust humans but loves to hang out near his family. He also enjoys being pet and held. His foster is working on leash training, but his ideal home would have a fenced-in yard for his energetic zoomies. Hunny is both crate trained and housebroken. Hunny will need a loving family with the patience to help this boy learn to trust people. He has so much potential and we can’t wait to find him the perfect home. He is available for adoption.
